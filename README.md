# 手牌重排 / Hand Sort

## 中文说明

本 Mod 允许你通过**拖动手牌**来自由调整其显示顺序，从而提升操作舒适度与视觉管理体验。
适用于Slay the Spire 2 StS2

### ✨ 功能特点

* 支持使用鼠标拖动手牌来改变排列顺序
* 调整仅影响**视觉显示顺序**，不会改变游戏内部的实际牌序
* 不影响抽牌、出牌、弃牌等任何核心逻辑
* 适用于希望更清晰管理手牌的玩家

### ⚠️ 兼容性说明

本 Mod **仅修改手牌的显示层逻辑**。
因此，与任何会修改手牌数据结构、排序逻辑或出牌行为的 Mod **不兼容**。

如果同时启用，可能会出现显示异常或逻辑冲突。

---

## English Description

This mod allows you to **reorder your hand cards by dragging them**, improving clarity and overall usability during gameplay.

### ✨ Features

* Drag and drop cards to freely rearrange their order
* Changes are **purely visual** and do not affect the actual card order in the game logic
* No impact on draw, play, discard, or any core mechanics
* Ideal for players who want better hand organization

### ⚠️ Compatibility

This mod only modifies the **visual layer of hand cards**.
It is **NOT compatible** with other mods that alter hand logic, card order, or gameplay behavior related to cards.

Using them together may cause visual glitches or conflicts.
